Sub-processors

Theramate Limited · Last updated: 18 April 2026

We use the following categories of sub-processors to provide the Theramate platform. Contracts impose appropriate data protection obligations (UK GDPR Article 28). This list may change; we will update this page and notify practitioners where our agreement requires.

Category Typical providers / role
Cloud infrastructure & database Supabase (hosted PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage, Edge Functions) — primary application and data hosting.
Payments Stripe, Inc. — card payments and Stripe Connect for practitioner payouts.
Email delivery Resend or equivalent transactional email provider — booking and account emails.
AI inference (optional features) AI inference providers (e.g. Groq or others configured in production) — drafting or structuring clinical notes only when a practitioner uses AI-assisted note features; content is processed to return draft text.
Analytics (optional) PostHog or similar — product analytics where enabled and consented as applicable.
Error monitoring (optional) Sentry or similar — crash and error diagnostics where enabled.

Specific vendor names and subprocessors in use for your deployment are confirmed in our agreements and privacy documentation. For questions: privacy@theramate.co.uk.
