## 💰 **HOW THE CREDIT SYSTEM WORKS**

### **Earning Credits** 📈
- **Client Sessions**: Earn credits by providing services to clients
- **Automatic Award**: Credits are automatically awarded after completed sessions
- **Rate Calculation**: Based on service type and duration
- **Standard Rates**: ~30 credits for 60-minute sessions

### **Spending Credits** 💸
- **Peer Treatment**: Book treatment sessions with other practitioners
- **Platform Features**: Use credits for premium platform features
- **Automatic Deduction**: Credits are deducted when booking sessions
- **Insufficient Credit Protection**: System prevents overspending

### **Credit Management** 📊
- **Real-Time Balance**: Always shows current available credits
- **Transaction History**: Complete audit trail of all activities
- **Balance Tracking**: Current, total earned, total spent
- **Transaction Types**: `session_earning`, `session_payment`, `bonus`, `refund`

## 🔧 **STEP-BY-STEP ACCESS GUIDE**

### **Step 1: Access Your Dashboard**
1. Log in as a practitioner
2. Navigate to `/practice/dashboard`
3. Look for the **Credit Balance** card (gold coin icon)
4. Your current credit balance will be displayed

### **Step 2: View Detailed Credit Information**
1. Click on the **"Credits"** item in your navigation menu
2. Or navigate directly to `/credits`
3. View your complete credit balance and transaction history
4. Track your credit earning and spending patterns

### **Step 3: Book Peer Treatment**
1. Navigate to **"Peer Treatment"** in your menu
2. Or go directly to `/practice/peer-treatment`
3. Browse available practitioners
4. Select treatment type and duration
5. System will show required credits and your available balance
6. Complete booking - credits are automatically deducted

### **Step 4: Monitor Credit Activity**
1. Check your dashboard regularly for credit balance updates
2. Review transaction history in the Credits page
3. Track your credit earning from client sessions
4. Monitor credit spending on peer treatments

## 🚨 **TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **Can't See Credit Balance?**
1. **Check Role**: Ensure you're logged in as a practitioner (sports_therapist, massage_therapist, osteopath)
2. **Refresh Dashboard**: Refresh the page to load latest credit data
3. **Check Navigation**: Look for "Credits" item in your navigation menu
4. **Direct Access**: Try navigating directly to `/credits`

### **Credits Not Updating?**
1. **Complete Sessions**: Credits are earned after completing client sessions
2. **Check Transaction History**: Review `/credits` page for transaction details
3. **Refresh Page**: Credit balance updates in real-time
4. **Contact Support**: If issues persist, contact platform support

### **Can't Book Peer Treatment?**
1. **Check Credit Balance**: Ensure you have sufficient credits
2. **Verify Practitioner Status**: Both practitioners must be active
3. **Check Session Availability**: Ensure practitioner has available time slots
4. **Review Error Messages**: System will show specific error messages
