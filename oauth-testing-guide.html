🔧 Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Issue: OAuth callback fails

Solution: Check that redirect URLs are configured in Google Cloud Console and Supabase

Issue: Role not assigned

Solution: Check console logs for "RoleManager" messages and verify sessionStorage

Issue: User stuck on role selection

Solution: Clear browser storage and try again

Issue: Onboarding doesn't complete

Solution: Check form validation and required fields
