## ❌ **MISSING SPORTS THERAPIST-SPECIFIC FEATURES**

### **❌ 1. ITMMIF/ATMMIF/Pitch-Side Trauma Training Status**
- ❌ **ITMMIF/ATMMIF Qualification Tracking**: No specific tracking for sports therapy trauma qualifications
- ❌ **Pitch-Side Cover Finding**: No system for sports therapists to find pitch-side cover for football/rugby matches
- ❌ **Trauma Training Status**: No display of pitch-side trauma training status on profiles
- ❌ **Match Cover Booking**: No system for booking pitch-side cover for matches

### **❌ 2. Professional Body/Insurance Integration**
- ❌ **Membership Number Display**: No display of professional body membership numbers
- ❌ **Registration Number**: No display of professional registration numbers
- ❌ **Insurance Provider Integration**: No integration with insurance provider details
- ❌ **Professional Body Verification**: No verification of professional body membership

### **❌ 3. CPD Offering System**
- ❌ **CPD Course Management**: No system for offering CPD courses (up to twice a year)
- ❌ **CPD Enrollment**: No enrollment system for sports therapists
- ❌ **CPD Tracking**: No tracking of CPD completion
- ❌ **CPD Certification**: No certification system for completed CPD

### **❌ 4. Treatment Exchange Ratings System**
- ❌ **Client Ratings**: No rating system for sports therapists
- ❌ **Rating Display**: No display of ratings on Treatment Exchange page
- ❌ **Rating-Based Access**: No restriction of Treatment Exchange based on ratings
- ❌ **Rating Management**: No system for managing and displaying ratings

### **❌ 5. Enhanced Admin Support Features**
- ❌ **Advanced Diary Management**: Basic scheduling only, no advanced diary features
- ❌ **Enhanced Notes System**: Basic notes, no sports therapy-specific templates
- ❌ **Business Analytics**: Basic analytics only, no sports therapy-specific metrics
- ❌ **Client Progress Tracking**: Basic tracking, no sports therapy-specific progress metrics
